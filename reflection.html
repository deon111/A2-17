Group Reflection

Prestige Worldwide, a team comprised of Mya, Glenn, Deon and Aden. We have worked hard and harmoniously together on this group assignment. Here is our reflection on how we think we have performed as a team and what we learned.

When we started we were a bit unsure of where to begin, the first meeting was only attended by Aden and Deon, but with the 2nd meeting when Mya and Glenn were also able to join, Deon should good skills as a manager, evenly delegating the work amongst everyone. We communicated effectively with each other, and to assist each other with understanding of tasks and assistance where required. We had productive meetings in which we were able to discuss where we were in terms of our task progress and what the next step was. We were also able to give criticisms and constructive feedback respectfully and use the feedback to improve our work.

While communication was good, there is always room for improvement, on occasions there was missed messages and late responses. It was sometimes difficult to work around our individual work and personal life schedules, time management was not as good as it could have been, however with our hard work ethic, we mawe managed to complete the tasks to what we believe is the best of our abilities.

Furthermore, when team was created there were no ground rules established. Some rules were implicit and never clearly expressed. As rules hold each member accountable and we believe if these rules were established early on, it would have produced a better environment for the group to flourish.

One thing that surprised us as group during our discussion of the group reflection was that during our work process, there was no conflicts or disagreements. We were able to work out and solve any problems as a team and behaved professionally and respectfully towards each other. This was a positive outcome for the group.

Things that we have learned about working within a group is that time management is important and different team member have different skills. We were able to identify each team members skill sets early on my discussing and analysing each other’s profile from assignment 1. We have learned the value and importance of time management, and we can use this in the next instance of group work. As a team we have supported each other and worked hard together, working as a team is not always easy but we are proud of ourselves and the work we have accomplished.
